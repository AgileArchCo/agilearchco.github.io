Parking Guidelines

Welcome to Park Trent!!

Where convenience meets campus life

Rules

  • • Parking in the lots indicated for your reservation is important. Parking in restricted locations without permission is forbidden.
  • • For certain events, tests, and other time-sensitive activities, reservations can be made in advance. Please reserve your position in advance by using the pre-booking feature.
  • • Don't ever go over your allocated parking time.
  • • If you do not extend your time for your lot, you will be subject to penalty. (Notifications will be sent in advance)
  • • For tracking and administration purposes, the license plates of every vehicle must be registered with the app.
  • • Lots with accessibility spaces are available for individuals with valid permits. Misuse of these spots will result in penalties.

Pre-Booking Rules

  • • When you reserve your lot, make sure you get a booking confirmation. Your reservation is invalid if it is not confirmed.
  • • To avoid fines, cancellations must be made at least 15 minutes prior to the scheduled time.

User Responsibilities

  • • When registering and making a reservation, provide precise vehicle and user information.
  • • In addition to these rules, follow all parking policies and procedures on campus.
  • • Don't reserve more spaces than required or make a reservation without planning to park.
